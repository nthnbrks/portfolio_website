About Me

Howdy and welcome to my website! I'm Nathan, a computer science student and quantitative finance master's candidate at Texas A&M. I have a lot of disparate passions, and I hope that this website can serve as a way to categorize and display those passions and a little bit about what I've done to further myself over these past few years.

Here you can find links to connect with me further, my short list of computer science-related projects, my much longer list of written works, some skills and qualifications of mine, my one meaningful service project, and a little bit of extra fun for those with the time to be nosy and browse around.

So please, dear viewer, explore and allow yourself to understand me, as I put myself here to be examined by those beyond my knowledge or control. Feel free to send me an email or linkedin message to explain to me how and why my website sucks and how I may go about fixing/improving it. Have fun! Love you.
