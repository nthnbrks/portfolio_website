Writing Portfolio

My Writing

I’m currently an editor at The A-Line Magazine. This is my first semester as editor, as I spent my last 3 as a staff writer for the organization. I had no writing experience before this outside of school assignments. I believe the process of learning to write for an audience for the purpose of entertainment to have been an incredibly valuable source of development for me, both emotionally and professionally. I have had to expose my creativity, passions, technical skill and knowledge, public speaking ability, and time management skills to many people both directly and indirectly. I have been able to push my own boundaries and limits to write about a wide variety of topics in a wide variety of styles. A-Line has been the most rewarding part of my college experience, and I cannot wait to see how my time here continues.
